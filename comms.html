Department of Communications

Mission Statement

Dispatch is a very important role within law enforcement officers. As a dispatcher, you will relay information to officers on duty as well as help assigning them to certain calls. Dispatchers need to have a lot of skill and fast thinking. As dispatchers, we do not snap under pressure and we don't let our officers down!

Join the Department of Communications today!

The department is always looking for individuals dedicated to serving and protecting the public of San Andreas.
